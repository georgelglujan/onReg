Eligibility

To register, you MUST be a citizen of the United States of America; at least 6teen (16) yearsold; a resident of Guam; AND you must NOT be committed under a sentence of imprisonment; confined to a mental institution or judicially declared incompetent.

To register ONLINE, you must use a valid Guam Drivers License or a valid Guam Identification Card.
