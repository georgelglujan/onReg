Register to Vote

Qualifications

You must qualify in ALL of the following ways in order to register to vote in Guam.

  • You must be a citizen of the United States of America;
  • You must be at least 16-yrs old upon registering;
  • You must be a bonafied resident of Guam - having resided in any one village for at least 30 consecutive days prior to registering to vote in that village.
